Training

1-2-1

In order for you to reach your fullest potential and maximize results, this option is what we strongly recommend. Especially if you're seeking out to gain confidence in the weight's room. I have over 10,000 hours of face to face client experience so when it comes to dealing with getting the most out of an exercise I can set you up with the correct structure and feel to reap the full benefits of your program.

If you're dealing with a specific injury that you feel limits your oppurtunities in the gym don't worry there are always work arounds...

Your Journey Begins with a movement screen, despite sounding a little scary this is simply a way for me to collect the fundamental information on how your body naturally moves, its current limitations and capabilities. For example it's necessary to know what ranges of motion you have at your hips, knees and ankles in order for you to execute a squat efficiently.

We can also carry out other tests that would be specific to your goal...
VO2 max - Endurance
Rep max tests - Strength (appropriate for level)
Vertical Jump - Power.

I then go away and use all of this information to prepare your program for the coming months.

The first few sessions can be fairly tough and I will push you as much as necessary depending on your level and what your goal demands. My Philosophy is Consistency & Frequency before Intensity. Meaning get plenty of practice with the key exercises before starting to build up the weights.

Weigh in's will occur at the end of every week, dependent on your schedule of course.

Pictures will be taken every four weeks at weigh in time.

Strength/Fitness Testing will take place every 6 weeks. (If essential to your goal).

Most of all I believe 1-2-1 training should be a fun, immersive & fulfilling experience. The high of a good session is unparalleled and I hope that once you've finished your first session, you'll be eagerly anticipating the next!
