Training

1-2-1

In order for you to reach your fullest potential and maximize results, this option is what we strongly recommend. Especially if you're seeking out to gain confidence in the weight's room.

I have over 10,000 hours of face to face client experience so when it comes to dealing with getting the most out of an exercise I can set you up with the correct structure and feel to reap the full benefits of your program.
Your Journey Begins with a movement screen, despite sounding a little scary this is simply a way for me to collect the fundamental information on how your body naturally moves, its current limitations and capabilities.

For example it's necessary to know what ranges of motion you have at your hips, knees and ankles in order for you to execute a squat efficiently.
All of the data from the movement screen is used to prepare your program for the coming months.

The first few sessions can be fairly tough and I will push you as much as necessary depending on your current level and also what your goal demands.

My Philosophy is Consistency & Frequency before Intensity. Meaning get plenty of practice with the key exercises before starting to build up the weights.
  • Weigh in's will occur at the end of every week, dependent on your schedule of course.
  • Pictures will be taken every four weeks at weigh in time.
  • Strength/Fitness Testing will take place every 6 weeks. (If essential to your goal).

Online Training

If you are already accustomed to training with resistance equipment and are confident with your form on the fundamental lifts. However you feel as though you're missing something to unlock that next level of results. Then look no further! Online training is designed for you.
However it's not simply a case of finding the right program alone. It takes discipline and the correct mental attitude to tackle a training plan. I will help you with this in the 'Hands On' package.
Guidlines will be sent across to you about how to perform and video yourself performing a movement screen.

See 1-2-1 section for details of why you must perform a screen before starting a program.
Communication is key with the online training more so than with 1-2-1 because I am never in the gym with you. Accurate record keeping is essential with regards to training logs, food diaries and weigh ins which will be more frequent compared with the online program.

With technology being what it is today, I would recommend using your phone for all of these tasks. Especially recording your weights/reps in the gym. Use our handy mobile app to keep all your records in one place!
  • Weigh in's will occur daily first thing in the morning.
  • Food logs should be updated daily but they will be checked and feedback given once per week.
  • Pictures will be taken every four weeks under certain guidelines to be issued.
