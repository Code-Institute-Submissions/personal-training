How much weight can i actually lose over 12 weeks?

Of course the answer depends on a number of factors but the good news is if you're just starting out in your training journey, then it's likely to be more than the seasoned trainee. Some clients have acheived north of 10kg weight loss in this time frame.

We tend not to go by scales alone because really the optimal result requires you hanging onto what muscle you can whilst burning fat. So I personally like to check body fat percentage periodically to ensure muscle mass is kept at a healthy range.

How do i know which package is best for me?

Great question! Knowing your goals and any deadlines would be key here. If there is an aggressive deadline around the corner or you want to see the best results possible then i would definitely push for our gold package.

If you're new to the training game and are slightly worried about overdoing it then as a safer option i would recommend that most individuals try our silver package.

The right track program is really only designed as a nudge for experienced trainees. Similarly with the bronze package.

If in doubt you could always ring us up for a chat - 01234 567 890.

I have a back issue. Will this stop me from doing personal training?

All back issues are different and some will limit your options in the gym more than others. But there is always a work around for a given situation. This is exactly why we require you to perform a movement screen at the beginning of your journey. In order to asses what is currently off limits and if there's anything that we can do to improve the impaired movement.

Resistance training as a whole could be a little more risky, training modalities such as yoga are great for back issues but nothing is risk free.

In your situation i would say that you're better off training with a professional than without one. Be sure to seek out medical advice first, before embarking upon a training regiment.
