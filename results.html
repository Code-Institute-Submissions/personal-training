How much weight can i actually lose over 12 weeks?

Of course the answer depends on a number of factors but the good news is if you're just starting out in your training journey, then it's likely to be more than the seasoned trainee. Some clients have acheived north of 10kg weight loss in this time frame. We tend not to go by scales alone because really the optimal result requires you hanging onto what muscle you can whilst burning fat. So I personally like to check body fat percentage periodically to ensure muscle mass is kept at a healthy range.
